Payment Advisory

NB: Parents are strongly advised to pay full school fees before students report to school to avoid inconveniences. If unable to pay full fees at once, a minimum payment as indicated for specific classes must be made. Students with incomplete fees will not be allowed to attend classes.

UNEB Registration 2025

Every prospective S.4 and S.6 student must pay UNEB registration fees prior to being enrolled in a candidate class. The amount of money payable includes both UNEB registration fees and electronic passport photograph costs.

Registration Fees
  • S.4 Candidates: 260,000 UGX
  • S.6 Candidates: 260,000 UGX
  • Included in total term fees for candidate classes
Required Documents
  • 2 photocopies of P.L.E slips (S.4 Candidates)
  • 2 photocopies of U.C.E results slips (S.6 Candidates)
  • 1 photocopy of birth certificate
