Academic Excellence Redefined

At St Maliko High School, we offer comprehensive O'Level and A'Level programs designed to nurture intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and practical skills for real-world success.

Academic Structure

Comprehensive education from O'Level to A'Level

Ordinary Level

O-Level Program (S1-S4)

Four-year comprehensive program following the Uganda National Curriculum, preparing students for Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE) examinations.

Core Subjects:
  • Mathematics
  • English Language
  • Biology
  • Chemistry
  • Physics
  • Geography
  • History
Elective Options:
  • Entrepreneurship
  • Computer Studies
  • Religious Education
Advanced Level

A-Level Program (S5-S6)

Two-year specialized program offering Science and Arts combinations, preparing students for Uganda Advanced Certificate of Education (UACE).

Science Combinations:
  • PCM (Physics, Chemistry, Maths)
  • PCB (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)
  • BCM (Biology, Chemistry, Maths)
  • MEG (Maths, Economics, Geography)
Arts Combinations:
  • HGL (History, Geography, Literature)
  • LEA (Literature, Economics, Art)
  • HED (History, Economics, Divinity)
  • GEP (Geography, Economics, Politics)
