Our Legacy

A Legacy of in Education

Founded in 2018, St Maliko High School Hoima has grown from humble beginnings to become one of the most respected educational institutions in Western Uganda. Our commitment to quality education has remained unwavering through the years.

We pride ourselves on offering both O-Level and A-Level education with comprehensive Arts and Sciences programs. Our modern curriculum is complemented by state-of-the-art facilities and a nurturing environment that fosters academic and personal growth.
