Our Academic Programs

Comprehensive education designed for success

Ordinary Level

O-Level Program (S1-S4)

Comprehensive four-year program covering core subjects and electives, preparing students for Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE) examinations.

Mathematics English Sciences
Advanced Level

A-Level Program (S5-S6)

Specialized two-year program with Science and Arts combinations, preparing students for Uganda Advanced Certificate of Education (UACE).

Science Combinations:
  • PCM (Physics, Chemistry, Maths)
  • PCB (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)
  • BCM (Biology, Chemistry, Maths)
Arts Combinations:
  • HGL (History, Geography, Lit)
  • LEA (Literature, Economics, Art)
  • HED (History, Economics, Divinity)
